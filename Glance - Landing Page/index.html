Why Glance?

Imagine being able to define your purpose by asking the right questions to solve real-world problems. Imagine being able to create your path and move forward one step at a time. Imagine others joining you for a cause that unites everyone.


Now, imagine you could align your collaborative efforts real-time and be part of exciting projects, in a Glance.

PLAN

Plan ahead by breaking down everything you need to do into projects, milestones and tasks. Together called 'objectives' that can be organised by deadline and focus on what matters first.

SHARE

Reach your potential through collaboration. Involve like-minded people to your projects and share any objective with anyone and grow together without borders and boundaries.

PROGRESS

Define your part and time yourself. Understand your efforts and the time you spend on them. One step at time, move forward improving yourself on the way.

Define your part for the future

Take the first step. Question the world around you. Find answers to those problems.

Become the newer and better you

Progress by learning and execution, acquiring new skills on the way. Share objectives and lead others to do the same.

Sharpen your focus and widen your perspective

Be upto date real-time with the work of your passions. Always know what needs to be done and your the road you are creating for yourself.
