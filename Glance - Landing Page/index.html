Why Glance?

Imagine being able to define your purpose by asking the right questions to solve real-world problems. Imagine being able to create your path and move forward one step at a time. Imagine others joining you for a cause that unites you.


Now, imagine you could align your collaborative efforts real-time and be part of exciting projects, in a Glance.

PLAN

Plan ahead by breaking down everything you need to do into projects, milestones and tasks. Together called 'objectives' that can be organised by deadline and focus on what matters first.

SHARE

Reach your potential through collaboration. Involve like minded people to your projects and share any objective with anyone and grow together.

PROGRESS

Define your part and time yourself. Understand your efforts and the time you spend on them. One step at time, move forward improving yourself on the way.

Define your part for the future

Take the first step, and enquire the world around you for solutions to problems you want to solve. As you understand your role better, you can define your role each step along the way.

Become the newer and better you

Play your part, one step at a time. Progress by learning and doing, forming new skills on the way. Share objectives and help others achieve the same.

Sharpen your focus and broaden your perspective

Be upto date real-time with the work of your passions. Always know what needs to be done and your the road you are creating for yourself.
